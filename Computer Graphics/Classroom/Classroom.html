Arrow keys to move, 'a'/'d' to look left/right, 'w'/'s' to move up/down.

'm'/'n' to open/close the door.

'z'/'x' to move chair 1 back/forward, 'v'/'c' to move table 1 forward/back.

'f'/'g' to move chair 2 back/forward, 'j'/'h' to move table 2 forward/back.

'1', '2', '3', '4', '0' to toggle point light source 1, 2, directional light, background colour, and the ambient light.
